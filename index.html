A PERSONALISED APPROACH TO NUTRITION

Hey! I'm Gauri

Registered Dietitian & Nutrition Coach

I've spent years helping people navigate confusing diet trends and build habits that actually last. I understand what it's like to struggle with food choices, energy crashes, and the pressure of "eating right"—which is why I value simplicity, balance, and evidence over extremes.

There isn't one perfect way to eat. Real life is more nuanced. I focus on helping you move beyond restrictive diets, cut through the noise, and make thoughtful, informed nutrition decisions that work in your real world.
